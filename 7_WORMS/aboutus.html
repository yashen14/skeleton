About us

7worms was started by Robbie Van Der Walt in December 2015. After suffering a stroke in February 2013, he discovered the advantages and need for natural healing. Robbie started the healing process by taking only natural food herbs and spices and has recovered a great deal from this life changing event. That inspired him to start gardening again and had many setbacks with poor quality soil and therefore started a wormery.

Industry

Our worms are organically bred earthworms. Our earthworms receive the correct diet , they only get leafy greens such as:potato peels, carrot peels, etc. no sulfur based food or citrus or cooked food is fed to our worms. Our earthworms are bred in an moist environment between 0c and 25c.

Our compost ,vermicompost ,is the most nutritional most natural compost on the planet. Our compost can benefit crop growth more than conventional fertilizer. Our compost .vermicompost, allows the plants to absorb all the correct minerals in the soil. and therefor makes for a healthier plant and a better crop.
